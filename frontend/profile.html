Help Centre
How to book a campsite

Open a campsite, tap Book My Camp, enter your booking details, review the information on the confirmation page, then confirm the booking.

How to save a campsite

On the campsite details page, tap the heart icon. The heart will fill once the campsite has been added to your saved list.

How to cancel a booking

Go to the Bookings page, open the Active tab, and tap Cancel Booking. Cancelled bookings will move to History.

How notifications work

CampEase sends important updates such as booking confirmations and other activity notices to your Notifications page.

Weather and location guidance

Weather and exact location details are shown on the campsite details page to help you make better camping decisions.

About CampEase
What is CampEase?

CampEase is a campsite discovery and booking support platform designed to help users explore camping locations across the UAE.

What does it offer?

The platform brings together campsite browsing, weather visibility, exact location access, saved campsites, and booking support in one experience.

Project scope

CampEase focuses on improving campsite discovery and planning support. Some features, such as Trip Planning, were explored in the prototype stage only.

Plan Trip
Prototype-only feature

This feature was explored during the prototype stage and is not part of the current implemented CampEase system.

What it was intended for

The Trip Planning feature was designed to help users organise activities and prepare a fuller camping journey beyond the booking flow.
